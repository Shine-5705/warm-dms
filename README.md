# warm-dms

**Make cold outreach feel warm.**

A conversational agent that turns a goal — *"find me 20 seed VCs who back infra,"* *"reach the design hiring manager at Figma,"* *"email NeurIPS profs about my PhD application"* — into a researched shortlist, personalised messages with every factual claim traced to a source, and an optional physical gesture (printed letter, flowers, coffee) sent to the person's office. So they actually remember you.

## How it works

1. Tell it who you want to reach and why, in plain English.
2. It researches — posts, papers, funding, moves — and proposes targets with a reason for each.
3. It drafts a DM and a letter per person, grounded in evidence you can hover to verify.
4. You review, tweak, approve.
5. It mails the letter, hands you the DM to send, and pre-fills a vendor page if you want to add a gesture.

## Why it's different

Most outreach tools give you a list and a template. **warm-dms** gives you the *person*, the *reason* to talk to them, the *proof*, the *letter*, and the *gesture* — in the time it takes to drink a coffee.
